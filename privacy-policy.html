Privacy Policy for Cinder Chrome Extension

Effective date: April 28, 2026

Data Collection

Cinder does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

How Cinder Works

The extension takes the note you write and creates a link. That link is copied to your clipboard. When someone opens the link, the note appears and then self-destructs.

No server is involved. Your note exists only inside the link's URL fragment (the part after #), which never leaves your browser.

Permissions Used

  • storage — Remembers your last selected timer setting. No note text is saved.
  • clipboardWrite — Copies the generated link to your clipboard.

Third Parties

Cinder uses no third-party services, analytics, or advertising.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, this page will be updated. No data is collected, so no notifications are needed.
